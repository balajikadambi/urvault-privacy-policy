Privacy Policy for UR-Vault

Effective Date: 10-Dec-2025

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of the UR-Vault mobile application (the “App”).

1. Our Commitment to Zero-Knowledge Privacy

We have designed our App using a zero-knowledge security model. This means that all your sensitive data, including your password configurations and all other contents, are encrypted on your device. Only you have access to your secrets for the website or service, encryption key for backup/restore, and the device authentication needed to decrypt your data. We do not store any data on servers.

All data is stored on the device in encrypted format either in the keystore for password configurations or device file system for backup/restore. We cannot view, access, or decrypt the data stored in your device, even under subpoena.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Due to our zero-knowledge architecture, we do not collect, store, or have access to any of the following:

  • Your Secrets
  • Your Password Configurations
  • Your Encryption Keys
